Home health services are offered to those who have difficulty or limitations in leaving their home to find or secure medical assistance. Skilled nursing, physical therapy, speech therapy, occupational therapy, or home health aides are all available. These services are provided to those who request home health assistance. All our home health patients will remain under a physician’s care, supervision, and observation.

Medicare
This is for patients 65 years or older. Benefits are typically covered at 100%.

Private Insurance
Blue Cross Blue Shield, United Healthcare, Aetna, and other private insurance companies provide benefit packages to individuals that can cover up to 100% of the service.

Private Pay

Visits can range 2 to 4 times a week depending on the patient’s needs and physician’s direction.

Typical visits provided by a Nurse or therapist last 30–45 minutes.
