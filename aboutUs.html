ABOUT CAMILLIANS HOME HEALTH SERVICES, LLC

Located in Houston, Texas, our company has been in this business for more than 10 years. We bring more than 40 years of experience in home healthcare to our senior clients. Our staff is proficient in Spanish, Chinese, Tagalog, Vietnamese, and English.

Biographies

Maggie Tolles, Founder and Administrator

In 2011, Maggie founded our company where she is currently the president and CEO. Her vision is to build her company into a premier home health service provider dedicated to compassionate care and hospital-quality care at home.

Maggie has more than 35 years of experience in healthcare including 20 years at the UCLA Medical Center in Westwood California. She graduated from a medical school in Cebu City, Philippines. In the US, she worked in dialysis and found her way to the UCLA Medical Center, one of the world's leading dialysis centers.

In 1996, Maggie was awarded Nurse of the Year in UCLA’s outpatient sector for her work with dual kidney/liver pediatric cases. She worked for 10 years in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) at UCLA.

JAMES TOLLES, CFO

With his wife Maggie, James co-founded our company in 2011. He brings more than 25 years of expertise in computer engineering (hardware and software) along with his 20+ years of finance and investment experience. James looks to bring the latest in tech in terms of custom software applications.

He graduated from Williams College, Massachusetts in 1978 with a degree in physics and worked as an electronic/software engineer for 25 years, including 3 years in the Silicon Valley.

MARTIN LOPEZ JR.

CEO / ASSISTANT ADMINISTRATOR

Martin a former collegiate athlete turn healthcare professional has 12 years experience in healthcare. He is driven with the ambition to help as many people as possible. 5 years of pediatric home health experience with 1 year of hospice and 6 years of adult home health, Martin knows the importance of providing excellent, quality and professional help to patients at home.
