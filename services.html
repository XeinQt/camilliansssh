SERVICES

At Camillians Home Health Services, LLC in Houston, Texas, we employ a full-team approach. We work collectively with your physician to develop a plan of care exclusively designed to help you achieve your goals. Our services include:

SKILLED NURSING

  • Wound care for pressure sores or a surgical wound
  • Patient and caregiver education
  • Intravenous or nutrition therapy
  • Injections
  • Monitoring serious illnesses and an unstable health status

PHYSICAL / OCCUPATIONAL / SPEECH THERAPY

  • Your therapist will develop a comprehensive, goal-oriented treatment plan and discuss it with you during your initial evaluation.
  • We will communicate with you and your doctor throughout the entirety of your treatment. We will monitor and keep you up to date on your progress and healing process.

HOME HEALTH AIDE SERVICES

Home health aides help with activities of daily living (ADLs). The three main duties of a home health aide include:

  • Assistance with personal care
  • Light housekeeping
  • Companionship
